About Hammer Time

Hammer Time is an exciting game created by Drew Hamlett that tests your reflexes and timing skills. The goal is simple: smash as many targets as possible and try to last as long as you can!

The game keeps track of your high score and how long you lasted, so you can always try to beat your personal best. It's easy to learn but challenging to master, making it perfect for quick gaming sessions.

How to Play

Game Controls

  • Press Enter to start the game
  • Use your mouse or touchscreen to play
  • Click or tap to smash targets
  • React quickly to increase your score

Game Objectives

  • Achieve the highest score possible
  • Try to last as long as you can
  • Beat your previous records
  • Challenge your friends to beat your score
